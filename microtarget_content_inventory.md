# Microtarget Content Inventory

## Hero messaging
“Un conjunto de indicadores microterritoriales que reflejan la demanda de diferentes tipos de servicios, desde retail a servicios financieros pasando por restauración, entrega a domicilio, inmobiliario o planeamiento urbano.”

## Key claims
- Fine granularity (100 m grid). Indicators can aggregate to road segment, building portal, census tract, postal code, municipality.
- National coverage. Whole of Spain.
- Advanced analytics from open data. Mentioned techniques: machine learning, NLP, Monte Carlo simulation.
